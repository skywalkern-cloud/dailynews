AI needs a strong data fabric to deliver business value

AI is rapidly becoming a core component of enterprise operations, shifting from isolated experiments to everyday, mission‑critical deployments. By the end of 2025, roughly half of organizations are expected to use AI in at least three business functions—such as finance, supply‑chain management, human resources, and customer‑service—leveraging copilots, autonomous agents, and predictive analytics to drive efficiency, insight, and personalization. However, extracting sustainable business value from these AI initiatives hinges on a robust data fabric. A data fabric provides a unified, trusted, and governance‑enabled layer that connects disparate data sources, ensures data quality, and delivers the right information to AI models in real time. Without this integrated foundation, AI systems suffer from fragmented, inconsistent, or low‑quality data, which limits accuracy, slows time‑to‑insight, and raises compliance risks. Consequently, enterprises must invest in modern data‑management practices—including automated data lineage, master‑data management, and federated governance—to enable AI at scale. When the data fabric is strong, AI can reliably turn raw information into actionable intelligence, powering decisions across the organization and delivering measurable ROI.

**自我实现预言的运作机制** 自我实现预言的核心机制在于，预言或预测会影响人们的行为，从而使得原本可能不会发生的事情变成现实。这一机制在现实生活中有着广泛的应用场景，其中最贴近普通人生活的例子便是保险保费。当保险公司根据某些风险因素预测某个人更容易发生意外或疾病时，他们可能会收取更高的保费。然而，这种预测本身就会产生连锁反应：被收取高保费的人可能会因为经济压力而减少健康检查、降低生活质量，甚至放弃某些预防性措施。这些行为反过来会增加他们发生健康问题的风险，从而验证了保险公司最初的预测。 从这个角度来看，保险公司的高保费预测不仅仅是风险评估的结果，更成为了塑造客户命运的推手。预测者和被预测者之间形成了一种微妙的权力关系：前者通过预测获得了某种控制权，而后者则在不知不觉中被预言所塑造。维利兹教授强调，这种现象在社会各个层面都普遍存在，从金融市场的泡沫到社会歧视的固化，自我实现预言的逻辑无处不在。 ##
